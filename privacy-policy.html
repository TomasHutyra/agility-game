Privacy policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Bookdragon Development (Tomas Hutyra) handles information in connection with the Agility Game (available on Google Play). Effective date: June 7, 2025.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only email addresses, and only when voluntarily provided by you within the game interface. We do not collect IP addresses, location data, or device identifiers.

2. How We Collect It

All information comes directly from users through optional input fields in the game. There are no background data harvesting processes.

3. Technologies Used

The game uses Google AdMob for displaying advertisements and is built with Unreal Engine. We do not employ analytics SDKs, cookies, or automated tracking of any kind.

4. How We Use Your Data

Collected email addresses are used solely for gameplay improvement and optional communication features. No profiling or personalized advertising is performed.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third parties.

6. Data Storage

Data remains locally on your device and persists only while the game is installed or until you manually delete it.

7. Your Rights

You may decline email entry at any time without affecting gameplay. You may request access to or deletion of your data through the game settings or by contacting us directly.

8. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this policy. Updates will appear in-game or in the store listing.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions contact: tomas.hutyra@gmail.com
